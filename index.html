CareConnect

CareConnect is a structured digital intake layer designed to standardize patient request capture before escalation through existing nurse call and communication systems.

It enhances — rather than replaces — established infrastructure by introducing:

  • Structured request selection
  • Role-based routing logic
  • Time-sensitive prioritization support
  • Language-accessible intake options

The Clinical Workflow Gap

In many hospital environments, patient requests are transmitted through unstructured channels. This can result in ambiguity, role confusion, inefficient routing, and limited visibility into response performance.

CareConnect addresses this gap by standardizing intake at the source — before requests enter downstream communication systems.

Integration Model

  • Designed as a lightweight digital intake layer
  • Compatible with existing nurse call and messaging platforms
  • Supports API, webhook, or configurable routing logic integration
  • Does not replace established emergency or safety escalation pathways

Vendor Partnership Opportunities

CareConnect is positioned to enhance existing healthcare communication ecosystems by introducing structured intake intelligence prior to message escalation.

  • Enhances nurse communication platforms
  • Adds structured intake before task escalation
  • Supports cleaner analytics and response tracking
  • Introduces configurable role-based routing intelligence
  • Expands accessibility through language-inclusive request entry

Built From the Frontline

CareConnect was developed by a clinical workflow specialist with over 10 years of frontline nursing experience.

The system combines bedside insight with systems-level workflow thinking, designed to align with health system infrastructure requirements.
