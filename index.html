Who We Are

Crawford Solutions is a healthcare technology company focused on designing practical workflow systems for hospital care teams. Our work is informed by firsthand clinical experience and a systems-level understanding of how care is delivered.

We build tools that reduce friction in daily operations, support clearer prioritization, and help teams work more efficiently—without adding cognitive burden or disrupting established safety practices.

Design Principles

  • Efficiency with intention. Streamlining workflows to reduce unnecessary interruption while preserving clinical judgment.
  • Care-centered systems. Supporting care teams and patients through clearer, more equitable communication pathways.
  • Clinician-informed. Designed with direct insight into hospital environments, workflows, and real-world constraints.
  • Enterprise-ready. Built to integrate conservatively within existing clinical and safety frameworks.

CareConnect

CareConnect is a digital patient request and triage platform designed to support clearer request capture, improve visibility for care teams, and assist with prioritization of routine and time-sensitive needs.

The platform is designed to complement existing workflows, providing structure and clarity without replacing established safety pathways or clinical decision-making.
