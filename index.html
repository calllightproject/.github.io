CareConnect (prototype)

A digital patient request and triage layer designed to complement existing nurse call and care-team communication platforms.

What it does

  • Captures patient intent via structured options with optional free text
  • Supports prioritization of time-sensitive vs routine requests (safety-first)
  • Routes requests by role (e.g., licensed vs support)
  • Provides a status feedback loop (ack / on my way / completed)

Why it matters

  • Reduces ambiguity and “clarification loops”
  • Decreases relay burden and avoidable interruptions
  • Supports equitable access (language + accessibility)
  • Improves team situational awareness without adding alarm noise

Trust & safety posture

Early-stage prototype with an intentionally cautious design stance.

Safety-first

Designed to complement existing call-light workflows and established emergency pathways—not replace them.

Access & auditability

Room access can be gated (e.g., signed QR + active window), and key events can be logged for transparency.
