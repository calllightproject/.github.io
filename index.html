Our Approach

Crawford Solutions is a healthcare technology company focused on designing practical, systems-level tools informed by real clinical experience. Our work centers on reducing ambiguity, supporting equitable care, and helping care teams operate with greater clarity and efficiency.

We believe effective healthcare systems should support clinicians, not add cognitive burden. Every solution we design prioritizes safety, transparency, and alignment with established clinical workflows.

Design Principles

  • Clinician-informed. Designed by frontline healthcare professionals with a deep understanding of real-world hospital environments.
  • Equity-centered. Built to support patients with diverse communication needs, language preferences, and access barriers.
  • Systems-focused. Complements existing safety pathways rather than replacing them, integrating conservatively into clinical operations.
  • Operationally calm. Reduces unnecessary interruption, alarm fatigue, and back-and-forth communication.

CareConnect

CareConnect is a digital patient request and triage platform designed to standardize request capture, support prioritization of time-sensitive versus routine needs, and route requests by clinical role.

The system is designed to operate conservatively within hospital safety frameworks, providing clarity and visibility for care teams without introducing new risk pathways.
